Guide
Purpose

A premium YouTube music player — stream, organize, and control music from your lock screen or home screen.

Adding Music
  1. Tap + to reveal input.
  2. Paste a YouTube link (any format).
  3. Tap ADD — track appears in All Tracks.
Managing Playlists
  1. Go to Playlists tab → tap Create New Playlist.
  2. From any track, use the list-plus icon to add it to a playlist.
  3. Tap a playlist folder to view its tracks.
Video Preview Toggle

Tap the eye icon at the top to hide/show the video player. Audio keeps playing even when hidden.

Downloading Tracks

Tap the download icon on any track — the link is copied, and after a 3-second reminder you can open the converter site.

Installing the App

Tap "Install" in the banner at the top, or use your browser's "Add to Home Screen" option for the best lock-screen experience.

Tips

Do not refresh or close the tab while playing — playback will pause. Install the app to your home screen for the best uninterrupted experience.
